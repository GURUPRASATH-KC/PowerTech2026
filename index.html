ABOUT SYMPOSIUM

Igniting the Spark of Innovation

PowerTech 2026 is the flagship technical symposium of ABC Engineering College. We bring together the brightest minds across the nation to showcase their skills, exchange ideas, and push the boundaries of modern technology.

With a focus on futuristic engineering, Artificial Intelligence, and sustainable technologies, we provide a premier platform for students to transition from learners to industry-ready innovators.

  • Cutting-edge technical events
  • Industry networking opportunities
  • Exciting cash prizes & awards

TECHNICAL EVENTS

Compete, Innovate, and Conquer.

Paper Presentation

Present innovative ideas related to EEE, AI, IoT, renewable energy, robotics, and smart systems.

Circuit Debugging

Identify and solve faults in electrical and electronic circuits.

Technical Quiz

Quiz competition based on engineering, electronics, aptitude, and current technologies.

Project Expo

Showcase innovative student projects including automation, robotics, and IoT systems.

Coding Challenge

Competitive programming and problem-solving contest using C, Python, and Java.

PCB Design

Design efficient PCB layouts using professional engineering tools.

Robo Race

Autonomous and manually controlled robot racing competition.

Line Follower

Robots automatically navigate through complex designed paths.

Arduino Hackathon

Develop innovative embedded systems and automation projects.

Tech Connections

Engineering-themed connections game inspired by TV quiz concepts.
